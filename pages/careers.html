CAREERS

Kushagramati Analytics is an environment that fosters innovation and empowers our employees to bring out the best of their abilities. We believe in the highest order of business ethics and encourage a value based mutually beneficial relationship amongst all our stakeholders. At Kushagramati, your opportunities to learn and grow are boundless and we’re looking for talented individuals to take that journey with us.

We are looking for the following:

ROLE EXPERIENCE POSITION(S) TYPE EXPECTED TO JOIN LOCATION
Project Manager 3-5 years 2 Full Time Immediate Bangalore

We are looking for committed, passionate and hardworking Project Managers with experience working on Big Data and SaaS projects. This position requires well-rounded individuals who focus on details, thrive in a fast-paced environment, know how to cultivate collaborative working relationships, can communicate effectively to executives and drive projects to completion.

Who you are

Key requirements of the role are as follows:

  • Experience delivering, and successfully closing projects
  • Successful track record of delivering projects on time and on budget
  • Successful track record of delivery success rate of > 95% with a project margin >50%
  • Ability to run and monitor multiple projects simultaneously
  • Formal approach to project execution (has applied a methodology) from "interest to invoice"
  • Customer savvy and sales friendly
  • Be seen as a leader on the team and projects
  • Ability to anticipate and get ahead of potential problems is key

Additional soft skills we are looking for:

  • Professional and Confident presence
  • Polished, collaborative and consultative demeanor
  • Ability to be firm when required
  • Good presentation and facilitation skills
  • Takes pride in delivering customer success, without compromising project-financial goals
  • Desire to learn new technologies and willingness to understand the business problems customers are trying to solve

What you will do

Project/Program Management

  • Take ownership of all commercial delivery of services from a project administration and director perspective across numerous concurrent projects
  • Capable of overseeing multiple concurrent projects and modifying management approaches depending on project size and duration
  • Projects may range from a single individual for 2-3 weeks to 6 month engagements of multi-person teams
  • Execute and enforce Project reporting and milestone tracking
  • Work closely with the account management teams to manage handover from sales to delivery team
  • Work closely with the account management team to ensure accurate reporting on projects and keeping all relevant parties up to date with project activity and success
  • Manages scope and mitigates risk across projects.

Practice Accounting

  • Take ownership of margin and revenue targets for projects
  • Timely billing, timesheet approval
  • Determine appropriate revenue recognition, ensures accurate invoicing, and monitor receivables for all projects under his/her direction.
  • Manages to and achieves revenue goals set for projects
  • Accurately forecasts revenue, profitability, margins, bill rates and utilization across projects
  • Sets expectations upward regarding revenue and profitability projection.

Are you passionate about this opportunity, but worried that you don’t have 100% of the experience we’re looking for? We still want to hear from you! Apply online and let us know why you would make a great addition to the Kushagramati team.
